Realization of Off-Shore PC-Clusters

Project :

Standardization and modernization of a fleet of 15 geophysical acquisition vessels

Time period :

2011-2014

Environment/domain :

Standardization and modernization of processing clusters (budget of approximately $5 million over 4 years).

Tasks :
  • Standardization of processing clusters
  • Search for new hardware/computer to improve performance without compromising space, power and cooling
  • Construction of a prototype to test performance in the context of the modernization of computing clusters
  • Responsible for the implementation of processing clusters
  • Implementation of the procedure and optimization of the installation of processing clusters
  • Report on the installation and configuration of processing clusters


The success of the standardization was rewarded with an award in late 2012.


Onboard computing power has increased from 112 cores to 864 cores.
Storage capacity has increased from 384 Gb to 1.4 Pb.
